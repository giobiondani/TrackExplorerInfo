Privacy Statement

Last updated: December 27, 2025

TrackExplorer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Statement explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Storage

  • No data collection by us: The app does not collect, store, or send any personal information to us, our servers, or any third parties.
  • All data stays on your device: All user data—including hike history, distances, elevation details, times, and all related information—is stored only on your local Windows device.
  • Local storage only: We use standard Windows storage mechanisms (such as local files or app data folders) that remain entirely under your control.

Optional OneDrive Integration

  • User-initiated sync only: If you choose to save or sync your data to OneDrive (via built-in Windows features or app options), that data is stored in your personal OneDrive account.
  • Microsoft handles OneDrive: Any data stored in OneDrive is governed solely by Microsoft's privacy policies. See: Microsoft Privacy Statement. We do not access, process, or control data stored there.

No Sharing or Analytics

  • No third-party sharing: We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to anyone.
  • No analytics or tracking: The app includes no analytics tools, crash reporting, or tracking services that send data externally.

Your Rights and Control

  • Full control: You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time within the app or by managing the local files on your device.
  • Uninstall to remove: Uninstalling the app removes all local data it created (except any files you manually saved to OneDrive or elsewhere).

Changes to This Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this web address, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
